FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really a one-time payment with absolutely no hidden charges?

Absolutely. You pay $499 (setup) + $25 (maintenance fees) — monthly, and that is the end of it. $25 monthly fees, no hidden charges. Your chatbot is yours for life. We believe in honest, transparent pricing.

How long does it take to build and deliver my chatbot?

We deliver within 48 hours of receiving your completed AI Knowledge Base Form. The more detailed your information about products, services, and FAQs — the faster and smarter we can build your bot. Most bots are delivered even sooner.

What information do I need to provide to train the AI?

The AI Knowledge Base Form guides you through everything — your business name, products, pricing, services, FAQs, business hours, contact info, tone of voice, and specific answers you want the bot to give. No technical knowledge required. Just fill it in plain language.

Which platforms does the chatbot work on?

We build for WhatsApp Business, your website (WordPress, Wix, Shopify, custom-built — any platform), and fully custom AI bots for other use cases. During onboarding, simply tell us which platform you want your bot on.

Will the bot sound like my brand or like a generic robot?

100% custom. Every NexaBot is built to match your brand's tone, language, and personality. It will not sound like a generic AI. Your customers will feel like they are talking to a knowledgeable member of your own team.

What if I need changes after the bot is delivered?

Minor updates are covered by your one-time $60 maintenance fee. If you need significant new features or major expansions later, there may be a small additional charge — but we will always discuss this openly and honestly before doing anything.

Is my payment secure? What payment methods are accepted?

All payments are processed 100% securely through PayPal — the world's most trusted payment processor. You can pay via your PayPal balance, debit card, or credit card. Your financial information is never stored by us at any point.
